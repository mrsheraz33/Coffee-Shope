Our Story

Founded in 2010, Brew Haven began as a small coffee cart with a big dream - to bring exceptional coffee to our community. What started as a passion project between two college friends has grown into a beloved local institution.

We source our beans directly from ethical farms around the world, roast them in small batches to highlight their unique characteristics, and prepare each cup with care and precision.

At Brew Haven, we believe coffee is more than a drink - it's a ritual, a moment of connection, and an opportunity to slow down and savor life's simple pleasures.
